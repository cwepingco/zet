Admission of New Students


All applicants for the degree programs shall pay a P150.00 (may be changed without prior notice) entrance examination fee; pass the entrance examination given by the University and meet the following qualifications:

1.1 Graduate of any public or DECS recognized private high school;

1.2 Physically and mentally fit to study;

1.3 Possess a good moral character as certified by the High School Principal.

Individual colleges and campuses may institute their own requirements specific to their programs provided these requirements are endorsed by the University Administrative Council and approved by the Board of Regents.
