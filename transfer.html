Admission of Transfer Students


Transfer students from other schools and colleges/universities are required to comply with the following requirements:



Submit the following:


> certified true copy of transcript of records;

> GPA should be 2.00 or better for programs with licensure/board examination (approved per BOR Res.No 51 s 2005);

> Honorable Dismissal;

> two (2) copies of 1x1 ID Pictures;

> Certificate of Good Moral Character certified by the guidance councelor/ dean from the last school attended;

> passing the required examination

Individual colleges and campuses may institute their own requirements specific to their programs provided these requirements are endorsed by the University Administrative Council and approved by the Board of Regents.
